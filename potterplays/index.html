Project Description

At PotterPlays we seek to analyze the open source screenplays of the magical series Harry Potter.

The goal of the project is to conduct textanalysis on all 8 of the Harry Potter screenplays to determine whether there is a correlation between the Social Classes found in the series and the diction utilized by each Social Class.
