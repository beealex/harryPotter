Project Description

At PotterPlays we seek to analyze the open source screenplays of the magical series Harry Potter.

The goal of the project is to conduct word analysis on all 8 of the Harry Potter screenplays to discover how gender is represented through the scriptwriter's diction.

Project Plans

***Still in the planning stages. Subject to change. ***

  • Conduct OCR on the PDFs of the screenplays and convert to .txt files.
  • Research the role of gender in the open source screenplays.
  • Research the infamous gender tweets relating to gender in the series.
  • Utilize Antconc to pull out phrases and how many times they are mentioned. This can be used to analyze various characters and their gender role/description.
  • Potentially develop a personography and/or character mentions
  • Incorporate important timeline of events (book releases, production dates, movie releases)
